## ① PROBLEM
- Sustainability information is **scattered and inconsistent** across many sources — shoppers can't research a product at the point of purchase.
- **Greenwashing**: "eco-friendly" marketing claims are frequently unverified or misleading.
- No quick, **objective way to compare** a product against greener alternatives.

**Existing Alternatives**
- Manual Google searches · brand's own marketing pages · scattered eco-labels (confusing, unstandardized) · niche apps like Good On You (fashion-only) · guessing.

## ② CUSTOMER SEGMENTS
- Environmentally-conscious online shoppers (Gen Z / millennials).
- Sustainability-minded parents buying household goods.
- Corporate/ESG procurement teams (B2B roadmap).
- Educators & students learning about responsible consumption.

**Early Adopters**
- Zero-waste & r/sustainability communities · eco-influencers · climate-conscious young professionals · university sustainability clubs.

## ③ UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION
**"See past the greenwashing — an honest, evidence-backed sustainability score for anything you buy, in seconds, with greener swaps."**

**High-Level Concept:** *"A CarFax + nutrition label for product sustainability."*

## ④ SOLUTION
- **Multi-agent AI** that researches a product across 3 dimensions (materials, brand ethics, packaging/end-of-life) in parallel.
- Returns a transparent **0–100 Sustainability Score** with a plain-English breakdown.
- Recommends **2–3 greener, purchasable alternatives**.
- Every claim backed by **live web citations** (anti-greenwashing).

## ⑤ UNFAIR ADVANTAGE
- **Agentic architecture** (LangGraph multi-agent + live evidence) is hard to replicate with a simple chatbot.
- **Transparency-first**: citations + a defensible scoring rubric build trust competitors' marketing pages can't.
- Improves as it aggregates analyses; brand-agnostic (no incentive to favor advertisers).

## ⑥ REVENUE STREAMS
- **B2C:** freemium (X free scans/mo) → premium subscription (unlimited + history + alerts).
- **B2B:** API licensing for e-commerce/marketplaces; ESG procurement dashboards.
- **Affiliate:** ethical-brand affiliate links on recommended alternatives.
- **Data/Reports:** anonymized consumption-trend insights for sustainability researchers.

## ⑦ COST STRUCTURE
- **Technology:** LLM API (Gemini Flash), Tavily search API, cloud hosting (Render + Vercel).
- **Development:** AI/LangGraph engineer, full-stack dev, UI/UX.
- **Operations:** evidence-quality validation, customer support, marketing.

## ⑧ KEY METRICS
- Products analyzed / week · Avg. analysis latency (<20s) · % analyses with ≥3 citations · Alternative click-through rate · "Changed my purchase decision" rate · Returning users.

## ⑨ CHANNELS
**Acquisition:** SEO ("is X brand sustainable?") · Reddit/eco-communities · TikTok/Instagram demos · university partnerships · Product Hunt.
**Distribution:** Web app → browser extension (roadmap) → e-commerce API integrations → mobile app.
